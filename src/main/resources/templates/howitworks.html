Flexibility

Make your own schedule! Those looking to get hired can pick up as many shifts as they want without running into overtime limits.

Restaurant owners and managers will be able to adjust shifts as needed. Sidework will help them manage full-time workers during slow business periods and last-minute hires whenever the holidays come around.

Convenience

Sidework brings the power to the people. Search for long-term job opportunities or make some quick cash by covering a restaurant who needs a worker immediately.

Managers will now have a platform to connect directly to potential employees without having to spend money posting on other advertising platforms. This allows managers the opportunity to advertise on their own schedules.

Expand Your Profile

Workers can partake in a broad range of experiences in techniques and practices at different venues and connect with people in the industry who can help them develop and expand their career.

Managers can even recruit some of the untapped talent that they might have never known they needed!
